Information Technology in Vardhaman College of Engineering Vardhaman College of Engineering offers a comprehensive B.Tech in Information Technology program, focusing on software development, database management, web technologies, cloud computing, machine learning, and cybersecurity. The program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in technology and engineering, preparing them for careers in the IT sector. The college emphasizes ongoing and emerging technologies, ensuring that students are well-prepared to meet industry demands. The college's B.Tech Information Technology program is a four-year full-time undergraduate course with an intake of 120 students. The total fees for the course is 5,60,000 (5.6 lakhs) for the entire four-year duration. The fees structure includes tuition fees of 5,60,000 distributed across four years, with each year comprising 1,40,000. Hostel fees are optional and range from 70,000 to 75,000 per annum if availed. Students may be eligible for scholarships based on merit and category (SC/ST students may receive full reimbursement from government). The college's placement cell helps students secure good jobs with high salary packages. The college's infrastructure, including well-equipped laboratories and sports centers, supports the learning process. For those interested in pursuing a B.Tech (Lateral) program in Information Technology, Vardhaman College of Engineering offers a 3-year undergraduate program designed for diploma holders and B.Sc. graduates. The program operates in full-time on-campus mode and is popular among diploma holders seeking to upgrade their qualifications and gain a B.Tech degree from a NAAC A++ accredited institution with excellent placement records and strong industry connections.
